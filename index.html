Chatable

Chatable is a chat client developed by Howard Stark. It is currently in the development phase, and there isn't any estimate on when it will be officially released. It was started as a small time chat client, but it has gotten much larger than that. It has now been under development for close to two months.

Features

  • Auto-updating
  • Usernames
